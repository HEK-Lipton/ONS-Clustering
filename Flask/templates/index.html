How to use:

This website clusters lower tier local authorities together based on household data from the ONS Census.

Select the categories you want to cluster on by using the checkboxes in the box to the right.

When you submit your categories the data will be clustered as requested. This can take between 10-30 seconds depending on how many categories you have selected.
